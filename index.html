The Product that Permitted the Great Recession

Beginning Middle End

1994

Blythe Masters, of JP Morgan, created the first Credit Default Swap.

This instrument allows fixed income investors to buy default protection on any bonds or loans.

Beginning Middle End

Early 2000's

CDS's were loved worldwide as investors could hypothetically take on limitless risk

in junk bonds while reaping the high coupons and passing on the risk to CDS

sellers. Banks could lend out more money to receive juicy fees while having little

regard for the underlying credit risk.
